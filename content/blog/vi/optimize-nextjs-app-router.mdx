# Toi uu Next.js App Router cho blog ca nhan

App Router de scale rat tot neu cau truc route va component ro rang.

## Cach to chuc route

- Tach list page va detail page ro rang.
- Dung dynamic segment cho slug.
- Tao generateStaticParams cho cac bai da biet truoc.

## Toi uu render

- Giu page detail la Server Component.
- Han che logic client khong can thiet.
- Dung metadata cho tung bai de toi uu SEO.

## Ket luan

Khi route, data va metadata duoc to chuc dung, blog nhe va de mo rong hon.
